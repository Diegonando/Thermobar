This folder contains files for various other functions available in Thermobar. 

viscosity.ipynb compares viscosity calculations performed in Thermobar to the supporting spreadsheet of Giordana (1 sample at a time), and their matlab code. 
Reads from Viscoity_Giordano.xlxs

oxide_to_wtperc.ipynb compares our oxide to element calculation to an EPMA output with both oxides and element wt% (Ox_to_element.xlsx)
